When custom makes sense

  • You have a manual or spreadsheet process that wastes time
  • You need a simple tool just for your team or clients
  • You want fewer errors and clearer tracking
  • You need role-based access or approvals
  • You plan to grow or reuse the tool long term

When it doesn't

  • You need a complex, multi-module system
  • You require public e-commerce or payments
  • You need a mobile app or offline access
  • You're not ready to define the workflow yet
  • You just need a one-off report
