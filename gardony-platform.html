Gárdony Platform

A modern municipal portal that brings information, services and the community together in one easy-to-manage system.

Client
Municipality of Gárdony, Hungary
Year
2025
UX/UI Design Frontend Directus CMS Integration Web Platform Admin Panel Docs & Training
01

Challenge

Gárdony's previous website was hard to update, difficult to navigate on mobile and scattered across multiple tools. The municipality needed a unified platform to communicate effectively, publish documents, manage events and enable online services.

02

Solution

We built a secure, scalable platform on Directus CMS with a clean, accessible design. Content is structured for reuse, editors can update everything easily, and residents can find news, documents, events and services in just a few clicks.

03

Result

The new platform increased engagement, simplified operations and improved transparency. Staff save time, residents get faster answers, and the municipality now has a future-proof foundation for digital services.
